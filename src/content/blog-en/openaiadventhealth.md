## The Essential Purpose of LLM Application in Healthcare

The true value of AI in healthcare extends beyond mere automation—it's about reallocating the time freed up to focus on human-centric care, the core role of healthcare professionals. AdventHealth, a leading US healthcare provider, has integrated OpenAI's technology to achieve this balance, reducing administrative burdens while elevating the quality of patient care.

According to an official OpenAI report, AdventHealth champions 'Whole-person care,' aiming to maximize time for healthcare workers to engage individually with patients by leveraging AI as a supportive tool.

## Specific Implementation Approach: Reducing Administrative Burden

One of the biggest challenges healthcare professionals face is the immense workload from documentation and data management. AdventHealth is advancing by incorporating LLMs into these workflows through the following approaches:

1. **Streamlining Documentation**: 
AI assists with routine tasks like creating and summarizing clinical records, reducing the time doctors and nurses spend on computers and fostering an environment where they can better focus on patients.

2. **Rapid Information Extraction**: 
By quickly extracting and organizing necessary information from vast medical data and records, AI accelerates decision-making processes.

These initiatives free medical staff from administrative tasks, enabling a comprehensive approach that includes not only physical care but also emotional support for patients.

## Towards Achieving "Whole-Person Care"

AdventHealth's pursuit of 'Whole-person care' means treating not just the illness but the patient holistically—considering their physical, mental, and living environments. As AI handles administrative tasks, healthcare providers can dedicate more time to patient interactions, resulting in deeper empathy and more attentive care.

In this way, LLM implementation serves not as a cost-cutting measure but as a strategic infrastructure to maximize the value of human-led care.

## Summary: Perspectives for Developers to Note

The key takeaway from this case is that AI implementation KPIs should not be limited to 'shortening work time,' but should focus on 'what high-value human activities have been enabled as a result.' In fields like healthcare, which demand both advanced expertise and human touch, LLMs are highly effective as 'support tools to restore humans to their inherent roles,' rather than replacements.
